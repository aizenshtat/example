# Crowdship Contribution f4a6aa73-573d-46c3-b61c-f4caa1f20dc5

## Title

Add inline anomaly replay to Orbital Ops mission view

## State

agent_queued

## Route

/mission

## User Problem

Today, when a signal drop anomaly is selected on /mission, operators cannot replay the lead-up inline. They need a way to review what happened immediately before the anomaly while staying on the mission screen and preserving their current mission context.

## Goal

Enable operators to inspect the lead-up to a selected signal drop anomaly directly within the /mission screen, without leaving the current mission context or changing the current filters and layout.

## Acceptance Criteria

- When a signal drop anomaly is selected on /mission, an inline replay appears within the anomaly details area on the same screen rather than navigating away or opening a separate full-screen experience.
- The inline replay preserves the operator’s current mission context, including the existing craft and time-window filters, selected mission view layout, and the currently selected anomaly.
- By default, the replay opens focused on the anomaly lead-up using a narrow fixed window of the 30 seconds before the signal drop through the moment of the anomaly.
- In v1, operators can play and pause the replay and scrub within the available replay timeline to inspect the lead-up without losing their place on /mission.
- If a non-signal-drop anomaly or other object is selected, no inline replay is shown and existing mission view behavior remains unchanged.

## Non-Goals

- Introducing replay support for anomaly types other than signal drop in this change.
- Adding advanced replay controls in v1 such as speed selection, frame-by-frame stepping, annotations, or side-by-side comparison.
- Changing global mission filters, mission layout, or navigation patterns as part of the replay feature.
- Defining cross-screen replay entry points outside the /mission experience.
